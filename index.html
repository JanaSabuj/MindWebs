About The Event

The event is a two-day fiesta of a technical and cultural extravaganza, we hope to keep the audience glued to their seats and wanting for more.

The first day comprises of a live session of IOT where the audience will be made to familiarize with an Arduino project. Which will be followed by speeches from motivational gurus and industry related stalwarts helping the audience to venture ahead in respective fields. Business orientations, internships lectures are to follow helping the newbies figure out more about the nooks and corners of the system. And to drain their stress off we intend to have a short recreational event from some eminent social media influencer.

The second day is more about the interaction of the audience with the stage, keeping them jolting on their seats and clearing all the doubts they have buzzing in their curious minds. There will be speaking slots by two relevant personalities of the modern day industry field to give the audience an updated insight.

Last but not the least, the finalists of the hackathon will be challenged again for the final glory, the will be put to test in front of the business panelist who will be monitoring their every action, the ones emerging victorious will be offered internships or exciting offers keeping in mind their approach to solving problems. Thus we will end our fiesta with grandeur.
